FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RxFlow, security, implementation, integrations, and pricing.

What is RxFlow?

+

RxFlow is a cloud-based e-prescription platform that helps healthcare providers create, sign, manage, and deliver digital prescriptions securely and efficiently.

Can I send prescriptions directly to pharmacies?

+

Yes. RxFlow supports direct pharmacy delivery, allowing providers to send prescriptions electronically while tracking fulfillment status.

How does the AI Prescription Assistant work?

+

The AI assistant analyzes patient information, medication history, allergies, and prescribing patterns to provide recommendations, dosage suggestions, and interaction alerts.

Is patient data secure?

+

Absolutely. RxFlow uses encryption, access controls, audit logs, and secure cloud infrastructure to protect sensitive healthcare information.

Does RxFlow support electronic signatures?

+

Yes. Providers can securely sign prescriptions electronically before delivering them to patients or pharmacies.

Can patients access prescription history?

+

Yes. Patients can securely access prescription records, medication history, and refill requests through the patient portal.

Can I import existing patient records?

+

Yes. RxFlow supports importing patient records and historical prescription data to simplify migration.

Is there a free trial available?

+

Yes. All new customers can start with a free trial to explore features and evaluate the platform before upgrading to a paid plan.
